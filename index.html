Key Features

Manage your personal finances with precision using our comprehensive budget management tools.

Expense Tracking

Easily add and categorize your expenses. Input the amount, select a category, and specify the date to keep track of all your spending in one place.

Visual Analytics

Get a clear breakdown of your expenses through interactive charts and graphs. Understand where your money is going with dynamic pie charts and bar graphs for each category.

Budget Limits

Set daily, weekly, or monthly budget limits for different categories. Stay on track with your financial goals and avoid overspending with timely notifications.

Real-time Summary

View up-to-the-minute summaries of your spending with real-time updates. Know exactly how much you've spent and how much of your budget remains.

Custom Categories

Create personalized expense categories to organize your spending in ways that make sense for your lifestyle. Add, edit, or remove categories as needed.

Cloud Sync

Access your financial data across all your devices with secure cloud synchronization. Your budget information stays up-to-date everywhere you go.

About Budget App

The Budget Management App is designed to help users effectively manage their daily, weekly, and monthly expenses. It enables users to track their spending across various categories, set budgets, and visualize their expenses over time.

Our Budget App provides a comprehensive solution for personal finance management. It helps you gain control over your finances, monitor spending habits, and work toward your financial goals with intuitive tools and visual analytics.
Anyone looking to manage their finances better can benefit from our app, including students, professionals, families, and small business owners. It's user-friendly and adaptable to different financial needs and goals.

Technologies Used

Our Budget App is built using modern technologies to ensure a smooth, responsive, and secure user experience.

Flutter

User interface built with Flutter, providing a smooth and responsive experience on both Android and iOS platforms.

Dart

Programming language used to develop Flutter apps. Dart's fast performance and compilation enhance the app's speed and efficiency.

Java Spring Boot

Backend built with Java Spring Boot to provide RESTful APIs for handling user data, budgets, and expenses.

MySQL

Robust relational database used for storing user data, expense records, categories, and budget limits.

Cloud Integration

Data synchronization across multiple devices with secure cloud storage ensures access to expense records anywhere.

Secure Authentication

Protected user authentication ensures your personal financial data remains private and secure.
